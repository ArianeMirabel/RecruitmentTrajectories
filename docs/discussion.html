In any case the density-dependence mechanisms maintaining forest diversity are lost after disturbance (this is why punctual recruitment is lower).When the disturbance is low: recruitment limitation because the recruited community is nested within the existing one and the recruitment is close to rendom. When the disturbance is high there is some selection as found for Molino and this is clearly an acquisitive strategies selection.
